Why?

Sugar is the new drug. In the modern day, sugars are too readily available and can be found in a variety of drinks or foods. This app, The Sugarfree Project, aims to help you easier keep track of the sugars you intake daily, and help with controlling it. By controlling sugar intake, we believe that you will live healthier, and also lower the risks of diseases like diabetes.

In addition, in order to give you an incentive to track your daily sugar intake, we also plan on adding a pet feeding system. You can use our remaining daily sugar intake quota to feed you pet. It will thrive with you!

The Sugarfree Project, your fun sugar tracker.

Features

One-tap Tracking

We collect beverage data from convenient stores like 7-11 and add them to our database. When you use our app, you can easily search for existing products and add it to your tracker. No barcode scanner needed. No guesswork.

The application will also display your recent and favorite drinks for faster tracking. For users on the iOS platform, you can easily access our app through the notification center and Force Touch. Tracking has never been easier!

Friendly Interface

The app is here to help you. You can easily see how much sugar you have intake in any single day. It will help you visualize your improvement as well as give you some great advice.

We will sometimes recommend similar drinks with less sugar content to help you on your journey. Moreover, you can set achievable goals for yourself and gradually improve!

Cute Pets Along the Way!

We know that it is boring and time-consuming to track everyday, so in addition to our easy tracking functions, we plan on adding some cute pets that you can feed and nurture along the way. The pets will thrive when you do!
